These are the sources of my creation and the deeper meaning of this website. I chose the mental model to build a Growing or Stretched to build an art website. Due to Jacob's Law, users spend most of their time on other websites, so I created buttons that are similar to the design language in the current software to guide users. It is very natural for users to click on the colorful buttons. This then leads to a page of randomly generated lines, which I call "rivers". Each time you click on the river button, a new line will grow, and the faster you click, the faster the "river" will be created. In addition, if the user clicks on an area other than the button, some of the "rivers" will randomly die out. This page has some decompression function, similar to pressing bubble wrap.In the process of experiencing this page, I believe the user will feel the unique life force of the river and lake. A line seems to emerge from the center as if it has life, just like the human civilization that grows and thrives by the river; at the same time, the death of the river will also take away a piece of human civilization, and I hope that human beings can remember to protect the river we depend on while enjoying the convenience brought by the river. In many civilizations, the important rivers are compared to mothers, so we can have no heroes, but we cannot have no "mothers".
